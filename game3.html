PAC-MAN



It is a normal PAC-MAN game played by one player .
The purple ball figured the player , it moves using the arrow .
The green ball help the player to kill the enemy(colored ball) ,
by tranform them to scared (babyblue) which can kill them .
Killing the enemy give a push in score to win rapidly .
The game end if the player achieve score equal 274 ,
or any enemy kill him ( other colored ball ) .
To restart the game click on reset button .
